Introduction¶

The dataset selected is no show appointments of may 2016. This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row.

The data type is categorical data
Charts could be used are bar, pie and line charts.

The columns will be used are:
PatientId: The ID for each Patient
AppointmentID: The ID of Appointment for each patient
Gender: Patient's Gender
ScheduledDay: The day the patient set up their appointment
AppointmentDay: The day of patient's appointment
Age: Patient's Age
SMS_received: Is patient Receive SMS massege for their appointemnt day
No-show: Is patient not showed up to their appointment or showed up

Questions

  1. How many times patient show up and not show up, Is there is big difference between them??
  2. What is gender of patient is the most and what showing up statue for each gender?
  3. Proportion of Patients recevied SMS massege?
  4. Is if SMS massage recevied affect on patient to show?
  5. What is most Age stage of patient and what is the show up statue for each age stage?
  6. What is Neighbourhood have most patients and what is the show up statue in each Neighbourhood?
  7. What is period between schedueled and appointment days and is if period long affect on patient show up?
